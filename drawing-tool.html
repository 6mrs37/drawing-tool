Instructions

To draw a new line, first click (and release) on the dot the line starts from. Then, move your mouse to the place the line is supposed to go to. Click. A line will be drawn with the current hue, saturation, lightness, and line width attributes. To change the hue, saturation, lightness, or line width select "edit old lines". Click on one endpoint of the target, then click on the other endpoint. The line should now be selected. Moving the hue, saturation, lightness, or line width sliders should change the respective attribute.
